Rules:
1. Player rolls the dice by clicking the roll button.
2. Player can then select the dice by clicking on them.
Once the dice are selected the player can choose to
keep them by clicking the keep button. Points are
then added to there current hand score.
3. Player can then decide to keep rolling the dice or the
Player can complete there turn by clicking the hold button,
in which case there current hand score is added to there score.
4. Points are determined by the following:
If 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, and 6 are all rolled together = 1500 pts
Three 1's = 1000 pts
Three 2's = 200 pts
Three 3's = 300 pts
Three 4's = 400 pts
Three 5's = 500 pts
Three 6's = 600 pts
Individual 1's = 100 pts
Individual 5's = 50 pts
5. If a player rolls and there are no potential points available
it is the next players turn and no points are added to there score.
6. Game continues until a player reaches 10,000 points.
Good Luck!
